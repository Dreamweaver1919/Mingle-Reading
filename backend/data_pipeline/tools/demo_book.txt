Chapter 1

Alice stepped into the reading room and found Bob waiting beside the window. "You came early," she said, trying to hide how nervous the silence made her.

Bob laughed and pointed to the stack of letters on the desk. He said the room remembered every secret, and Alice wondered whether memory itself could become a witness.

Chapter 2

The next morning, Alice refused to burn the letters. Bob argued that truth would hurt everyone, but she believed fear had already ruled them for too long.

When the bell rang, they stopped speaking. Alice kept one page in her pocket and decided that the future would have to judge what courage meant.
