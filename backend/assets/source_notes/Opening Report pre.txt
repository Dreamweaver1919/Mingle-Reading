Core Pain Points in Digital Reading
High Abandonment Rate
96% of college students give up on classics due to lack of explanation or
chapter summaries.
Short Attention Span
Average reading time per e-book is less than 3.5 hours; completion rate
remains below 15%.
Complexity Barrier
Abandonment rate exceeds 80% for works with complex narratives or rich
historical contexts.
Comprehension Struggle
50% of students struggle to understand deep textual connotations without
assistance.
Focus Loss
58.33% of readers find it difficult to maintain focus during long-form reading
sessions.
Desire for Connection
Readers desire a personified, opinionated companion, not just a dictionary or
generic AI summary.

Reader Demand Analysis
Guiding Deep Thinking (35%)

Proactive Highlighting & Interpretation
Readers want AI to identify and highlight noteworthy passages.
Personified Reading Companion
Desire for opinionated interaction, not cold dictionary-style

Anti-Spoiler Mechanism & Proactive Bubble Trigger
Dual-Layer Anti-Spoiler System
Data-Layer Filtering:
Every chunk tagged with positional metadata (chapter
index, section index, token offset).
Hard scalar pre-filter restricts candidate set to chunks with chapter index ≤
reader's current chapter.
Filter enforced at database level before any similarity computation—deterministic
guarantee.
Both
ChromaDB
and
Qdrant
implement metadata pre-filter natively, no custom
infrastructure required.
Prompt-Layer Constraint:
System prompt includes explicit high-priority
instruction on narrative awareness boundary.
Character has no awareness of events beyond reader's current chapter, cannot
draw on external plot knowledge.
Reader's current chapter position from
SageRead
frontend serves as
single
source
of truth for both layers.
Proactive Bubble Trigger Algorithm
During knowledge base construction, each chunk scored for emotional salience
by base LLM.
Trigger condition:
Reader pauses at high-salience passage beyond configurable
dwell-time threshold (15-30 seconds).
Output constrained to short first-person inner monologue expressing character’s
experience at that moment.
Grounded in RAG context available up to reader's current chapter, held
deliberately brief.
Design principle:
Strict restraint—interventions rare and well-timed, feels like
character confiding naturally.
